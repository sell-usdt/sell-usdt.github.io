Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) – Sell USDT

Is Selling USDT Safe?

Yes. Selling USDT is secure if you use reputable exchanges or P2P platforms with escrow and KYC/AML protections.

Where Can I Sell USDT?

USDT can be sold on most global CEXs, P2P platforms, DEXs, and via crypto ATMs.

How Fast Can I Get Paid?

Settlement is typically instant to minutes, depending on method and platform.

Are There Any Fees?

Fees depend on the platform and payout method, but are always displayed before confirming a sale.

Do I Need Verification?

For fiat payouts, most platforms require identity verification (KYC). For crypto swaps, not always.

What Are the Risks?

Use trusted platforms and beware of scams in P2P transactions. Check the reputation of buyers and always use escrow.
